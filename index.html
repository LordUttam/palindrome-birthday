Is your Birthday Palindrome?

Enter your birthdate and we will tell you if your birthdate is a palindrome.

This app checks your birthdate in 4 formats dd-mm-yy, mm-dd-yy, dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yyyy, yy-mm-dd, yyyy-mm-dd e.g. if your birthdate is 21 May 1995, then app will check for 210595, 052195, 21051995, 05211995, 950521, 19950521
